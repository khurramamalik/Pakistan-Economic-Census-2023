This dashboard is made utilizing Claude Fable 5 on publicly available Economic Census data from PBS and spatial boundaries from OCHA

Pakistan Bureau of Statistics Economic Census: Data source is https://www.pbs.gov.pk/pakistan-bureau-of-statistics-economic-statistics-production/

Administrative boundaries: OCHA/COD 2022 (admin level 1 & 2). Azad Kashmir, Gilgit-Baltistan and Islamabad are not included in the published district tables. 

Pakistan Economic Census contains district tables for establishments & workforce by PSIC major category and by unit type

Number of economic establishments and employment by Pakistan Standard Industrial Classifications are in PSIC Number.csv and PSIC Employment.csv, respectively, which contains cleaned data by province and district for further analysis by researchers.
